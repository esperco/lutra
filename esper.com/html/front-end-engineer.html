JOBS

Front End Engineer

We're looking for a front-end developer to help our users better manage their time. Esper is currently a small team, so you'll be involved in every aspect of product design, planning, and development.

The Stack

We currently use Typescript, React, LESS, and Bootstrap. We don't require prior experience with these technologies, only a willingness to learn. We're open to experimenting with new tools, but you should also be comfortable working with the codebase as it's currently written.

Requirements

  • You have a good grasp of Javascript fundamentals.
  • You can write clean, testable code (and actual tests).
  • You have an eye for good design and pay attention to the little things.
  • You're willing to take ownership of a feature and make sure it ships.
  • You've thought a lot about time management and how to improve it.
  • You're okay with color purple.

Bonus Points

  • You've developed user interfaces on mobile.
  • You've worked in a startup environment or have side projects of your own.
  • You have experience with OCaml (which we use on the backend) or other functional programming languages.
  • You enjoy a good game of Bughouse.

For more information or to apply, send us an e-mail.
