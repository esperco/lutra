Charts and Analysis

Activity Grid

This calendar grid shows the total number of events, the aggregate duration of those events, and your scheduling fragmentation for each day of the month.

Fragmentation measures whether your events are scheduled together in blocks (i.e. back-to-back meetings) are are scattered (fragmented) throughout the day.

Event Durations

This histogram groups your events by how long they take and shows the aggregate duration and frequency of those events for a given time period.

Frequent Meeting Attendees

This horizontal bar chart lists the people who are most frequently invited to events on your calendar. It shows both the aggregate duration and the number of events for each person.

Guest Domains

This pie chart shows which domain names are the most common among guests invited to events on your calendar and the most common guests for each domain.

For the purposes of this pie chart, time is divided evenly among all guests invited to an event. Filtering out certain domain names will re-allocate time in the chart among the domain names still included in the chart.

Label Duration Over Time

This bar chart shows the total number of hours allocated to each category over fixed intervals of time -- e.g. how many hours do you spend on Sales Meetings each week?

Label Allocation Percentage

This pie chart shows percentages for how you allocate time between different categories -- e.g. what percentage of your time was spent on Sales Meetings between November 1 and December 1?

If an event was assigned multiple labels, the time for that event is divided evenly between each visible label for the purpose of calculating percentages. Divided time will be labeled with a unit of "adjusted hours".

Label Percentage Over Time

This stacked bar chart shows how the percentage of time you allocate between different categories changes over a fixed interval -- e.g. how did the percentage of time you spend on Sales Meetings change between November and December?

This chart will use adjusted hours if an event was assigned multiple labels.

Working Hours Availability

This calendar grid shows uninterrupted blocks of free time during your working hours for each calendar day.

We consider working hours to be 9AM to 6PM in your local timezone by default but will adjust the period in the chart if your calendar frequently has events scheduled outside that period.
