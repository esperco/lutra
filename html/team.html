Esper was founded to help executives build better companies. We realized that by reducing the amount of time and resources spent on scheduling, executives could spend more energy on solving problems, and less on dealing with them.

Together, Joe Lonsdale, founder of Palantir and now at Addepar and Formation 8, and Andrew Lee, founder and former CEO of JamLegend, co-founded Esper to tackle this efficiency problem. Our team brings together a strong technical foundation and top tier investors passionate about changing the way we work.
