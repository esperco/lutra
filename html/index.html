    Supercharge your Inbox

    Esper communicates between your calendar and inbox so you can schedule and perform tasks faster and more easily.


    Create events directly from your inbox

    View multiple calendars and create events directly from your inbox. Simply click “Create Event” and pick a time... all within your inbox!


    Link notes and emails to events

    Connect private notes and emails so you and your Executive have immediate context on a task or upcoming event. No need to switch between tabs or search for old emails. Everything you need to complete a task on one screen.


    Find the perfect time

    Compare your Executive’s calendar with saved preferences at a glance to find the perfect time. Preferred times are overlayed onto your Executive’s calendar. Easily and quickly find the perfect time.


    Effortlessly work across time zones

    Arrange long-distance phone calls and meetings from your inbox without a timezone map. When creating the event just select time zones for the Executive and guest. Guest invites will accurately remind guests of the event and calendars will display both times when you hover over the event.


    Effortlessly communicate with Executives

    Use Esper to keep track of your Executive and stay updated on their tasks


    Enhanced 24/7 Communication

    The Esper app streamlines communication between an Executive and their Assistant. Executives can send voice or typed recordings, make adjustments to appointments and view the status of all their concurrent tasks with the Esper iOS app. All communications from the Esper app feed directly back into the EAs inbox.


    Read your Executive’s mind

    Executive preferences allow Assistants to record and easily view the ideal times to arrange different types of events. If an Executive requests coffee with friends on a certain date, the Assistant will be able to see the Executive’s preferred times to have coffee overlapping with the Executive’s existing calendar.


    Never miss a beat

    Esper Agenda keeps Executives aware of upcoming events and Esper Task List keeps Assistants up-to-date on concurrent tasks. Esper Agenda and Esper Task List can be automated to be sent every day or on-demand at the click of a button.


    Zip through tasks with workflows

    Work on multi step projects without missing a beat. Esper workflows allow Assistants to complete tasks that have specific steps or details with ease.
