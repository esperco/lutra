WORK FOR US

Esper strives to make the world run a little smoother. All of us are committed to this mission, and are making changes in the ways that we can.

As a start-up, everything around us is constantly moving – and so are we. Yet however hard we work during the day, we understand the importance of sleep and wellbeing. That’s why we start at 10 AM each day, why we cater in local food for lunch and dinner, and why we took a team trip to half dome. Plus, eating together gives us a chance to dive into conversations about wizardy, Star Trek, and our misguided teenage haircuts.

Functional programming (i.e. OCaml) underlies our technical approach, and allows our small team of engineers to create a product that can support millions of users. However, we still rely on Javascript, Java, and Obj-C – we believe in the right tool for every job.
