Gmail Sidebar State

This setting controls how the Esper sidebar is displayed in Gmail. You can always minimize and maximize the sidebar on a per-thread basis unless you choose not to display the sidebar at all. Task notes will still be visible even if the sidebar is hidden.


Google Calendar Sidebar State

This setting controls how the Esper sidebar is displayed in Google calendar. You can always minimize and maximize the sidebar on a per-event basis unless you choose not to display the sidebar at all.


Compose Toolbar

This adds a toolbar when composing Gmail replies that lets you insert canned responses and information about linked events.

Copy Selection Button

This shows a button that allows you to quickly copy text into the Task Notes when you highlight text from an e-mail in Gmail.
